Velvet Haze

Velvet Haze is a powerhouse in the punk rock scene, known for her raw, unapologetic sound and fierce dedication to social activism. Born and raised in the gritty streets of downtown Los Angeles, Velvet's music is a reflection of her rebellious spirit and desire to challenge the status quo. Her lyrics often tackle issues like inequality, political corruption, and environmental degradation, resonating deeply with a generation hungry for change. Velvet's onstage presence is electric, her voice a rallying cry for those who feel voiceless. With a signature look that combines vintage punk fashion with her own edgy twist, she is as much a visual icon as she is a musical one.

Beyond her music, Velvet is deeply committed to activism, using her platform to raise awareness and mobilize her fans. She regularly organizes benefit concerts, participates in protests, and collaborates with grassroots organizations to effect real change. Her social commentaries, often delivered through both her songs and public speeches, are sharp, insightful, and unflinchingly honest. Velvet's dedication to fighting the status quo has earned her a loyal following and respect from fellow activists and artists alike. Despite the challenges, she remains undeterred, continuously pushing boundaries and inspiring others to join her in the fight for a better world.

Slade Rebel

Slade Rebel is a charismatic punk rock artist whose high-energy performances and rebellious spirit have earned him a place among the legends of the genre. Hailing from the streets of London, Slade's music channels the raw power and edgy flair. His signature style combines snarling vocals, driving guitar riffs, and a theatrical stage presence that captivates audiences. Known for his platinum blonde hair and leather-clad outfits, Slade embodies the quintessential punk rock image, blending attitude and style with an unrelenting drive to push musical boundaries.

Offstage, Slade is just as dynamic, often found engaging with fans and speaking out on issues close to his heart. A staunch advocate for artistic freedom and individuality, he uses his platform to promote self-expression and defy societal norms. His lyrics frequently explore themes of rebellion, love, and the struggles of the modern age, resonating with a diverse fanbase. Slade Rebel's influence extends beyond music, as he continues to inspire a new generation of punk enthusiasts with his bold, uncompromising approach to life and art.

Amy Wilder

Amy Wilder is a powerhouse in the power punk scene, known for her electrifying performances and fierce, unapologetic sound. Hailing from the vibrant music scene of Seattle, Amy's music is a high-octane blend of punk rock energy and catchy, anthemic melodies. Her powerful voice and commanding stage presence captivate audiences, making every performance a raw and exhilarating experience. Amy's unique style, characterized by her bold fashion choices and brightly colored hair, sets her apart as a visual and musical icon in the punk world.

Amy Wilder is not only a dynamic force onstage but also a passionate advocate for animal welfare. Her lyrics often tackle themes of rebellion, self-discovery, and the fight against societal norms, resonating with fans who share her passion for change. Amy regularly engages with her community, organizing benefit concerts and speaking out on issues such as animal rights and environmental conservation. Her dedication to making a positive impact through her music and activism has earned her a loyal and diverse fanbase, solidifying her status as a trailblazer in the power punk genre.

Ruby Wise

Ruby Wise, a captivating singer-songwriter renowned for weaving enchanting melodies and heartfelt lyrics into mesmerizing compositions. Born with an innate talent for storytelling through music, Ruby Wise has carved out a unique space in the industry with her emotive voice and evocative songwriting.

From an early age, Ruby Wise displayed a passion for music, drawing inspiration from a diverse range of genres spanning from soulful ballads to folk-infused tunes. Influenced by luminaries such as Joni Mitchell, Sam Cooke, and Norah Jones, she honed her craft, crafting songs that resonate deeply with listeners on a personal level.

White Temple

White Temple's electrifying music is shaking both the stage and the gaming table. Born from the fiery depths of heavy metal and fueled by the passion of tabletop gaming, these modern-day bards are rewriting the rules of rock 'n roll.

Imagine a dimly lit room filled with the clatter of dice and the sound of shredding guitars. That's the world of White Temple. By day, they're crafting their epic music and by night, they're battling mythical beasts around the gaming table.

Their music is a heady concoction of thundering riffs, pounding rhythms, and lyrics that speak to the heart of every gamer. From the depths of dungeons to the heights of castles, their songs paint vivid landscapes where heroes rise, villains fall, and epic quests unfold.

Nathan Harris

Nathan embodies the soulful essence of contemporary music with his captivating vocals and heartfelt lyrics. Hailing from a small town with big dreams, Nathan discovered his passion for music at a young age, finding solace and expression through melodies and harmonies.

Drawing inspiration from his own life experiences and the world around him, Nathan weaves intricate tales of love and resilience into his music, resonating deeply with listeners of all ages. His emotive performances, marked by his velvety voice and genuine sincerity, have garnered him a loyal following. With each song, Nathan invites his audience on a journey of self-discovery and introspection, inviting them to feel deeply and connect with the universal emotions that define the human experience.
